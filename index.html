FAQ

How do I book a physiotherapist on Hello Physio?
Simply choose your service, pick a convenient time slot, add your address, and confirm the booking. You’ll get an instant confirmation and SMS/WhatsApp updates.
Will the physiotherapist come to my home?
Yes. Home visits are available in supported areas. The therapist brings basic equipment and follows strict hygiene protocols.
Are the physiotherapists certified?
Every professional on our platform is verified with registration numbers, qualification proof, and background checks. Reviews are monitored for quality.
Do I need any special equipment at home?
Usually no. A firm chair, yoga mat, and some space are enough. If a tool is required (like resistance bands), your therapist will bring it or suggest an inexpensive option.
Can I book the same physiotherapist for multiple sessions?
Absolutely. After your first visit, you can schedule follow-ups with the same therapist from the booking screen based on their availability.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?
You can reschedule up to 3 hours before the session with no fee. Late cancellations may incur a small charge to compensate the therapist’s time.
