FAQ

Do you offer home visits?

Yes — home visits are available in selected areas. Call to confirm availability for your pin code.

Is insurance accepted?

Some packages accept insurance; please check directly with the clinic.

What are your operating hours?

The clinic operates 24×7 for calls and appointments. In-person hours may vary by therapist schedule.

How many sessions will I need?

This depends on your condition. Most patients see improvement within 5–8 sessions with regular follow-ups.

Are the physiotherapists certified?

All physiotherapists are licensed professionals with verified qualifications and clinical experience.

Do you provide female physiotherapists?

Yes — female therapists are available upon request, depending on availability in your location.
